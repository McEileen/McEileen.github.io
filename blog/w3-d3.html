Day 3: Mistakes!

March 6, 2019

Recurse has several needlepoint hangings upstairs that have Recurse-y sayings on them, such as "You can do a lot in a day," "Make mistakes," and "Ask Anything." While I believe in the mottos, I sometimes have a hard time living them, particularly making mistakes, or admitting when I make mistakes. So for today's blog, I'm going to admit that I took the wrong subway line today. It happens. (And, dang, did it happen for awhile. That was a slow line.)
